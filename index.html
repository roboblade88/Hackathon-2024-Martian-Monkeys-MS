The Methane Problem

At this point, you may be asking "Where is the methane coming from?" And the answer, Anaerobic Digestion

By collecting human waste and other sewage in air-tight vats, enzymes can be added to create around 60% pure methane, then it can be filtered by it's density (lighter than air), then sent of to the methane combustion chamber as well as fuel for any combustion needed.
